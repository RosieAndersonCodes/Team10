About

Morgan Stanley Lates occur three times a year at Somerset House and provide one-off evenings of entertainment hosted by artists and curators alike. The first Morgan Stanley night is to occur on Wednesday 13 April and this will offer visitors the opportunity to be involved in a live sculpting class with the artist, Leeroy New in the courtyard of Somerset House. In addition to this, there will also be other activities available which relate to The Courtauld’s main exhibition of Van Gogh’s Self portraits.



Find out more

Visiting guidance​

Please note, it is free for anyone to attend all courtyard activities and after-hours exhibitions will also be free to anyone 25 or under. Fees will incur for any exhibition-related activities otherwise. Visitors aged 25 or under will also have the chance of receiving free entry to The Courtauld and Sony World Photography Awards exhibitions which can be found within the East and West wings of Somerset House.
